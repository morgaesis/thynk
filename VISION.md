# Thynk Vision

## One-Line Vision

Thynk is a blazing-fast, Markdown-native knowledge workspace for notes, structured planning, controlled documentation, and collaboration, built around user-owned data and progressive power.

## Product Promise

Thynk should feel instant for personal note capture, durable enough for long-lived knowledge, and structured enough for real project and compliance work. A user should be able to start with a simple note, then grow into links, properties, database-style views, tasks, automations, audit trails, sync, and collaboration without leaving the product.

## Differentiation

- Speed first: opening, searching, typing, and navigating should feel immediate.
- Local-first: users keep access to their data in open files, even when cloud sync exists.
- Markdown-native: Markdown is the primary content model, not an export format.
- Batteries included: core knowledge, planning, and documentation workflows are built in.
- Progressive complexity: simple capture comes first; advanced controls reveal when useful.
- Privacy-conscious AI: AI is optional, bring-your-own-key, and not required for core use.
- Collaboration without lock-in: teams can work together while preserving portability.

## Audience

Primary users are knowledge workers, developers, technical leads, operators, and compliance-minded teams who want one place for notes, internal documentation, lightweight planning, and controlled documents.

Secondary users include students, researchers, creative workers, and small teams that value local ownership, search, links, and daily-note workflows.

## Core Capabilities To Preserve

- Notes and documents stored in open Markdown with structured frontmatter.
- Fast full-text search across large workspaces.
- File tree, favorites, tags, recent notes, backlinks, and graph navigation.
- Daily notes, calendar views, templates, and project-oriented database views.
- Page properties, status fields, tasks, and lightweight automations.
- Page locking, audit trails, permissions, and notifications for team workflows.
- Optional AI for summarization, writing assistance, link suggestions, semantic search, and Q&A.
- Sync and collaboration designed around conflict handling, presence, mentions, and offline access.

## Non-Goals

- Do not rebuild as a cloud-only proprietary workspace.
- Do not make plugins necessary for basic notes, links, search, tables, tasks, or project views.
- Do not prioritize social/community features over private knowledge work.
- Do not make AI mandatory or vendor-locked.
